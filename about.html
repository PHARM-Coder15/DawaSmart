Empowering Pharmacy Students to Succeed

Dawa Smart was born from a simple idea: every student deserves access to quality, organized study materials without barriers.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide pharmaceutical students with accurate, exam-focused resources that bridge the gap between scattered notes and professional success. We believe in free, accessible education for all.

🌟 Our Vision

To become the most trusted digital companion for pharmacy students across Tanzania and beyond a platform where knowledge meets innovation, and every student can find the tools they need to excel.

As a former pharmacy student at Excellent College, I saw too many talented peers struggle with scattered, unverified study materials. Many faced supplementary exams not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked the right resources.

I combined my pharmaceutical training with my passion for technology to build DawaSmart. Every note, every calculation, every law is carefully curated from official sources and presented in a clear, exam-oriented format. My goal is simple: to help you pass your exams and become a licensed professional without the unnecessary stress.
